Text Styles:

H1: Main Heading or category start

  • Font-weight: 700(bold)

  • Font-size: 26px

  • Font-family: Sans-Serif(unless changed by more specific category)

H2: Subheading

  • Font-weight: 500

  • Font-size: 18px

  • Font-family: Zilla Slab

  • Font-size: 14px
